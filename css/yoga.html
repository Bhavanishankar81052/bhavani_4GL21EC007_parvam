Benefits of yoga

  • Flexibility: Yoga improves your flexibility and range of motion
  • Strength: Yoga strengthens your muscles, joints, and bones
  • Pain relief: Yoga can help relieve back pain, arthritis, headaches, and knee pain
  • Cardiovascular health: Yoga can improve your blood pressure and circulation
  • Digestion: Yoga can improve digestion by increasing blood circulation and massaging muscles
  • Posture: Yoga can improve your posture by strengthening your core, chest, and back muscles
